# Epic Himalayan Adventures Await

Discover breathtaking treks, hidden gems, and unforgettable journeys through India's most spectacular mountain destinations.

## Featured Destinations

### Kuari Pass Trek
Experience the "Lord Curzon Trail" with stunning views of Nanda Devi and Dronagiri peaks. Perfect for beginners seeking Himalayan adventure.

### Dharamshala Circuit
Explore the Dalai Lama's residence, trek to Triund, and discover the spiritual heart of Himachal Pradesh.

### Kota Road Trip
Journey through Rajasthan's heritage sites, temples, and cultural experiences.
