## Day 1: The Journey Begins - Jaipur to Kota via Divine Detours

**Date: October 2, 2025**

Our adventure began bright and early as we set off from Jaipur, embarking on what would become one of the most memorable road trips through Rajasthan. The approximately 250-kilometer journey to Kota promised not just a destination, but a tapestry of experiences woven through the heart of incredible India.

### First Stop: Garadia Mahadev Temple - A Spiritual Marvel

![Garadia Mahadev Temple](/travel/images/kota-trip/gallery/garadia-temple.jpg)
*The ancient Garadia Mahadev Temple perched on a cliff*

Our first major stop was the breathtaking **Garadia Mahadev Temple**, located about 30 kilometers from Kota city. This ancient temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is perched dramatically on a cliff overlooking the meandering Chambal River.

{{< alert "success" >}}
**Temple Highlights:** 1000+ years old, 500ft above sea level, panoramic Chambal River views, spiritual atmosphere with gentle breeze.
{{< /alert >}}

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Timings** | 5:00 AM to 7:00 PM |
| **Entry Fee** | Free |
| **Morning Aarti** | 5:30 AM |
| **Evening Aarti** | 6:30 PM |
| **Best Time** | Sunrise/Sunset |

### Culinary Adventure in Indra Bazar

After our spiritual experience, we headed straight to Kota's famous **Indra Bazar** to taste the legendary Kota Kachori. This wasn't just any ordinary kachori - it was a culinary revelation! The secret lies in the generous use of asafoetida (heeng) and the accompanying sweet khajur (dates) chutney that creates an authentic taste explosion.

We visited **Jodhpur Namkeens** in Gumanpura, where the kachoris are still served on traditional china plates, just like in the old days.

### Check-in at Hotel Clarks Premier

Our home for the next two nights was the elegant **Hotel Clarks Premier**, strategically located in Ridhi Sidhi Nagar. This 4.5-star property offered the perfect blend of luxury and comfort.

**Hotel Highlights:**
- **76 state-of-the-art rooms** including luxury suites
- **The Bridge Multi-cuisine Restaurant** for vegetarian delights
- **Falaknuma Rooftop Lounge** with city views
- **Swimming pool and fitness center**
- **Complimentary WiFi and breakfast**

### Evening at Oxygen Park (City Park)

As the sun began to set, we visited the magnificent **Oxygen Park**, also known as City Park. This 71-acre marvel, constructed at a cost of ₹120 crores, is truly Kota's green lung.

**Park Features:**
- **Entry Fee:** ₹100 per person (₹50 for students)
- **Timings:** 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM
- 3.5-kilometer central water canal
- Botanical garden with oxygen-rich plants
- Shikara boat rides (₹150 per person)

### Seven Wonders Park - The World in Miniature

We concluded our first day at the enchanting **Seven Wonders Park**, featuring scaled-down replicas of the world's most iconic monuments including the Taj Mahal, Eiffel Tower, and Great Wall of China.

**Park Details:**
- **Entry Fee:** ₹20 for adults, ₹10 for children
- **Timings:** 2:30 PM to 10:00 PM
- **Best Time:** Evening, when monuments are illuminated

## Day 2: Riverside Wonders and Cultural Immersion

**Date: October 3, 2025**

### Chambal Riverfront - A World-Class Heritage Experience

![Chambal Riverfront](/travel/images/kota-trip/gallery/chambal-riverfront.jpg)
*The magnificent Chambal Riverfront - world's first heritage riverfront*

The highlight of our second day was the **Chambal Riverfront**, proclaimed as the world's first heritage riverfront. This ₹1400 crore project stretches 6 kilometers along both sides of the Chambal River.

{{< weather-guide >}}
### 🎆 Musical Fountain Show
- **Height:** 42 meters with 154 pumps
- **Show Timings:** 8:00 PM and 9:00 PM daily
- **Experience:** Water, music, and lights choreography

### 🗿 Chambal Mata Statue
- **Height:** 40 meters in white marble
- **Feature:** Stunning water cascade effects
- **View:** Dominates riverfront skyline
{{< /weather-guide >}}

| Feature | Details |
|---------|----------|
| **Entry Fee** | ₹200 (₹100 students) |
| **Timings** | 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM |
| **Shuttle Cart** | ₹100 (6km stretch) |
| **Project Cost** | ₹1400 crores |

**Date: October 4, 2025**

## Travel Tips

### Best Time to Visit
- **October to March:** Pleasant weather, perfect for sightseeing
- **Avoid Summer:** April-June extremely hot

### Budget Breakdown (Per Person)
- **Accommodation:** ₹3,000-4,000 per night
- **Meals:** ₹500-800 per day
- **Entry Fees:** ₹400-500 total
- **Total:** ₹8,000-12,000 for 3 days

### Must-Try Foods
1. **Kota Kachori** with khajur chutney
2. **Rajasthani Dal Batti**
3. **Local namkeens** from Jodhpur Namkeens
