# Epic Himalayan Adventures Await

Discover breathtaking treks, hidden gems, and unforgettable journeys through India's most spectacular mountain destinations.

## Featured Destinations

### Kuari Pass Trek
Experience the "Lord Curzon Trail" with stunning views of Nanda Devi and Dronagiri peaks. Perfect for beginners seeking Himalayan adventure.
- **Duration:** 6 Days
- **Difficulty:** Easy-Moderate  
- **Season:** Oct-Apr

### Dharamshala Circuit
Explore the Dalai Lama's residence, trek to Triund, and discover the spiritual heart of Himachal Pradesh in this cultural journey.
- **Duration:** 4 Days
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Season:** Mar-Nov

### Chopta-Tungnath
Visit the world's highest Shiva temple and witness sunrise from Chandrashila peak. A perfect weekend getaway from Delhi.
- **Duration:** 3 Days
- **Difficulty:** Moderate
- **Season:** Apr-Nov
